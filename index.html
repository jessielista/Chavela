"Voy a gritar con toda mi alma para que el mundo sepa que estoy viva. Viva de tanto vivir. Viva de tanto amar."

Timeline of Chavela's life:

  • 1919 - Born in Costa Rica, San Joaquín de Flores.
  • 1936 - Left Costa Rica for Mexico at age 17 to expand her opportunities for a musical career.
  • 1950s - She became known within artistic circles, due in part to her performances in Acapulco, center of international tourism, where she sang at the Champagne Room of the restaurant La Perla
  • 1961 - Vargas released Noche Bohemia (“Bohemian Night”), the first of more than 80 recordings.
  • 1970s - She struggled with alcohol problems, however, and vanished from the stage.
  • 1990s - Launched a remarkable international comeback, championed by Spanish filmmaker Pedro Almodóvar.
  • 2012 - Died in Cuernavaca, Mexico.
